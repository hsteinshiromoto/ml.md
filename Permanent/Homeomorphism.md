# Homeomorphism

## Definition

**Definition**. A [[Continuous Function|continuous]] bijection $f : X \to Y$ whose inverse is also [[Continuous Function|continuous]] is said to be a _homeomorphism_. In other words, $X$ is _homeomorphic_ to $Y$.

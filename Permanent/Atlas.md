# Atlas

## Definition

**Definition**. An _atlas_ for a [[topological space]] $(M,\tau)$ is a family $\{(U_{\alpha },\varphi _{\alpha }):\alpha \in I\subset\mathbb{N}\}$ of [[Chart|charts]] on $M$ which [[Cover|covers]] $M$.

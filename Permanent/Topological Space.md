# Topological Space

## Context

## Statements

**Definition** ([[Tu, L. W. - An introduction to Manifolds|@Tu11, pp. 318]]). Let $X$ be a set and $\tau$ be a [[topology]] defined over $X$. The pair $(X, \tau)$ is said to be a *topological space*.

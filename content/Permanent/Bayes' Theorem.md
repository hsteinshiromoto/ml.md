# Bayes' Theorem

## Statements

**Theorem**. Let $(\Omega,\mathcal{F},P)$ be a [[Probability Measure|probability triple]] and let $A,B\in\Omega$ with $P(B) > 0$. Then,
$$
P(A|B)=\dfrac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)}\;.
$$

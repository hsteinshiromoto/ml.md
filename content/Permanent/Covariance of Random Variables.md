# Covariance of Random Variables

## Statements

**Definition**. The _covariance_ of two [[Random Variable|random variables]] $X$ and $Y$ with finite [[Expected Value|expectations]] is defined as
$$
\texttt{Cov}(X,Y)= E ((X − E(X))(Y − E(Y)))\;.
$$

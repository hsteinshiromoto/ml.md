# Local Criterion for Openness

## Lemma

**Lemma**. Let $(X,\tau)$ be a [[topological space]]. A subset $A$ is open in $X$ if and only if for every $p\in A$, there exists an [[open set]] $V$ such that $p \in V \subset A$.

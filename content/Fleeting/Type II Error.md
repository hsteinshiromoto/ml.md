# Type II Error

## Definition

Let $\phi$ be a [[test]], the [[Statistical Test|null hypothesis]] may not be rejected ($\phi(y)=0$) when it is in fact false ($\theta\in\Theta_1$). This error is said to be a _type II error_ or _false negative_ . ^be7a82
